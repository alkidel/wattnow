About

From July 2011 until January 2014, Watt Now was a resource for entrepreneurs, students, and the curious to discover the innovative cleantech world.  Every week, a blog article was written highlighting a new exciting cleantech company, organization, or idea.

 

About the author:

Alki Delichatsios is an energy and recycling enthusiast living in Paris and currently Product Manager at Lendopolis, a crowd-funding platform that allows people with little finance experience to easily and safely invest in renewable energy projects.

Prior, Alki was Head of Marketing for seven years at DualSun, a French company that developed an innovative 2-in-1 solar panel.

And before that, Alki worked with EarthSpark International, a non-profit developing clean energy solutions in Haiti and the International Energy Agency as a data analyst.  She also worked for five years as a business developer for AREVA in the nuclear recycling business unit.

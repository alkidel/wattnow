Solar Mosaic. Together we can all go solar.

Like 1BOG and the Qurrent concept, Solar Mosaic is another great example of energy 2.0 - in other words, rendering renewable energy accessible and collaborative.

Solar Mosaic provides individuals the opportunity to contribute to the construction of a solar project by investing in one or more "tiles" that make up a solar "mosaic" for as little as $100.   By investing in the mosaic, the individual contributes to the transition to clean energy and helps create local green jobs AND ... he or she will be paid back 100% through the revenue that the tile generates.  After all tile purchasers are paid back, revenue is then directed to fund other community solar projects.

Solar Mosaic is a clear demonstration of bottom-up opportunity for developing distributed renewable power.
